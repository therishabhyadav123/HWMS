Hospital Waste Management System

A full-stack platform for safer, smarter biomedical waste handling

MediEco connects hospitals, admins, and collectors in one workflow. From waste report creation to pickup, QR verification, tracking, and certificates — everything is managed in a single secure system.

Request Pickup View Services

Hospital Dashboard

Submit waste reports, attach photos, track status, download QR labels, and view notifications.

Admin Control

Assign collectors, update statuses, view analytics, export reports, and manage users.

Collector Workflow

View assigned pickups, scan QR codes, update progress, and send location updates.

How it works

  1. 1. Hospital submits a waste report with category, quantity, urgency, and location.
  2. 2. Admin reviews requests, assigns a collector, and monitors progress.
  3. 3. Collector scans QR at pickup, updates status, and logs proof of collection.
  4. 4. Certificates and compliance records are generated automatically.

Key features

  • • QR-based verification and label printing
  • • Live collector tracking and pickup history
  • • Notifications, alerts, and audit logs
  • • Multi-plan subscriptions and exports

Compliance & Safety

The system enforces proper segregation, tracks high-risk waste, and provides audit logs and certificates to help facilities comply with local biomedical guidelines and WHO standards.

Technology Snapshot

PHP + MySQL backend, QR integrations, Chart.js analytics, GPS updates, and a responsive dashboard UI for every role.
