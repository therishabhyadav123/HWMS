High-risk waste category

Infectious Waste

Pathogen-contaminated materials such as blood-soaked dressings, laboratory cultures, swabs, and bodily fluids. This category requires sealed containers, strict handling protocols, and regulated treatment to prevent infection spread.

Request Pickup Back to Services

Typical Items

Dressings, swabs, contaminated PPE, lab cultures, and blood bags.

Segregation

Use color-coded bags and sealed containers to prevent leakage and exposure.

Treatment

Autoclaving, incineration, or approved chemical treatment.

Handling Requirements

  • Wear gloves, gowns, masks, and eye protection.
  • Seal bags before transport; avoid overfilling.
  • Label containers with biohazard symbols.
  • Maintain chain-of-custody documentation.

Compliance Notes

  • Follow local biomedical waste rules.
  • Use licensed treatment facilities only.
  • Keep logs and pickup certificates.
